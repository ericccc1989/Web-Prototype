WE ARE WILD 2 FREE INC

We’re based on the NSW South Coast on acreage within the Mogo State Forest. Our home was destroyed in the Black Summer bushfires, but we’re rebuilding to continue providing a safe environment to release native animals back into the wild after rehabilitation.



Wild 2 Free is a registered charity and we are volunteers. Most of our funding comes from your donations.

We’re an endorsed DGR, donations over $2 are tax deductible in Australia.

We are licensed to care for wildlife in NSW under Wildlife Rescue South Coast.
